About us

We are a company that allows our customers to sell their houses on our site. We also allow the user to buy the houses other customers have added to our site. All these information is safe and stored in our database. Django has provided us with a really strong authentication system, that will protect us from any harmful threats.

Our focus is to help our customers buy the house they've been searching for. We also assign houses to the user. We have a simple user interface which will help the user. This project was given by our client, they wanted most of the features. House Buyers, have a feature where all the houses are dispalyed, and the customer can only buy when they are logged on.
